Tutorial

Before you start download and install the required fonts on your system (roboto & roboto slab).

Please follow the instructions in the given order to create your bubbles, otherwise unexpected results may occur.

  1. Choose desired template (domain, topic, staff, student, or job) by clicking on the tab "choose template"
    on the right.
  2. Choose the right domain color by clicking on "choose domain".
  3. Change the text by clicking on it.
  4. Change the picture by draging and droping a image file of your choice. In order to achieve best results,
    your image should be square and have a resolution of min. 1540 x 1540 pixels. State of the art
    smartphones do an adequate job. All staff or student pictures should be taken in front of a whiteboard.
    Use the whiteboard to individualise your picture or leave messages. Consider that a bit more than the
    lowest quarter of the image is cut off by the program. If you are adding a picture of a person, the eyes
    should be approximately at two thirds of the image height. If you are adding a picture of your topic use
    rather a full screen foto than a schematic illustration.
  5. Click on "download" and print the pdf. For best printing results and consistent colors please use
    Adobe Acrobat.
  6. Use the circular cutter to clip your bubbles to the specified size:
    D_domain = D_project = 130mm
    D_staff = 100mm
    D_student = D_job = 80mm


Please also consider further instructions.
