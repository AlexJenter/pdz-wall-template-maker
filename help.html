Tutorial

Before you start download and install the required fonts on your system.

Please follow the instructions in the given order, otherwise unexpected results may occur.

  1. 1. Choose desired template (domain, topic, staff, student, or job) by clicking on the tab "choose template"
    on the right.
  2. 2. Choose domain type by clicking on "choose domain".
  3. 3. By clicking on the text, you can enter your own text. If you want to add an image, select your image in your
    filebrowser and drag it onto the template. In order to achieve best reults, your image should be square.
    Consider that a bit more than the lowest quarter of the image is cut off by the program. If you are adding a
    picture of a person, the eyes should be approximately at two thirds of the image height.
  4. 4. Click on download and print the pdf.


Please also consider further instructions.
